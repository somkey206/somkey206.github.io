About Us

Sonam Choki's Vision

Welcome to Nature's Plate, where culinary artistry meets the breathtaking beauty of Bhutan's landscape. Founded by Sonam Choki, our restaurant near Kaja Throm offers more than just food - it's an experience that nourishes both body and soul.

Inspired by Bhutan's pristine nature, we've created a space where every dish tells a story of tradition, innovation, and respect for our environment. Our ingredients are locally sourced, our recipes honor Bhutanese heritage, and our presentation celebrates the natural world.
