My Culinary Journey

From dream to reality - the story of Sonam Choki

Hello, I'm Sonam Choki

Growing up in Bhutan, I was always surrounded by the breathtaking beauty of nature and the rich flavors of our traditional cuisine. Some of my fondest memories are of helping my mother and grandmother prepare meals using ingredients we gathered from our garden and the local markets.

My passion for cooking started at a young age, but it wasn't until I traveled abroad and experienced different culinary traditions that I realized how special our Bhutanese food culture truly is. I made it my mission to share these flavors with the world while staying true to our roots.

Nature's Plate is the culmination of years of dreaming, learning, and perfecting my craft. Located near Kaja Throm in Thimphu, our restaurant offers not just food, but an experience that connects people to Bhutan's natural beauty and culinary heritage.

Every dish we serve tells a story - of our land, our people, and my personal journey as a Bhutanese woman entrepreneur in the culinary world.

My Journey

Early Inspiration

1990s - Childhood

Learned traditional Bhutanese cooking from my mother and grandmother, developing a deep appreciation for local ingredients and flavors.

Culinary Education

2010-2012

Attended the Bhutan Institute of Hospitality and Tourism, specializing in Bhutanese and Asian cuisine.

International Experience

2013-2015

Worked in restaurants in Thailand and India, expanding my culinary horizons while always looking for ways to incorporate Bhutanese flavors.

Return to Bhutan

2016-2019

Came back to Thimphu with a renewed vision for showcasing Bhutanese cuisine, working at several top restaurants while developing my own concepts.

Nature's Plate is Born

2023

Opened Nature's Plate near Kaja Throm, combining my love for Bhutanese cuisine with inspiration from our country's stunning natural beauty.
